Declare It

State tool name, version, purpose, and task. Put the disclosure where the journal expects it.

Human Leads

AI can assist, but human authors keep intellectual ownership and editorial control.

No AI Author

Do not list AI as an author. Accountability, consent, and legal obligations are human responsibilities.

Verify Everything

Fact-check outputs, numbers, citations, and quotations before submission.

Protect Confidentiality

Avoid sharing unpublished manuscripts, reviewer files, or private data in public AI systems.

Image Caution

Image and figure policies vary by publisher. Check local rules before creating or editing visuals with AI.
