01. Minimal AI Author Checklist

Back to Gallery

Simple policy baseline synthesized from BMJ, STM, Wiley, and Taylor & Francis documents in this folder.

  • [+]Disclose AI use: tool name/version, why you used it, and how you used it.
  • [+]Place disclosure in Methods, Acknowledgments, or contributor statements as required.
  • [+]Never list AI as an author. Authorship remains human only.
  • [+]Human authors remain accountable for accuracy, references, and originality.
  • [+]Verify all AI outputs for hallucinations, bias, and citation errors.
  • [+]Do not upload confidential unpublished manuscript content to public AI tools.
  • [+]Check journal-specific limits, especially around image and figure generation/editing.
  • [+]Keep intellectual leadership and final decisions with the author team.
