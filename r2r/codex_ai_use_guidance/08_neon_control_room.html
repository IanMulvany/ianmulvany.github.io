08. Neon AI Governance Control Room

Disclosure Completeness

  • Tool and version documented
  • Purpose and workflow documented
  • Placement in required section confirmed

Accuracy and Citation Risk

  • Check for fabricated references
  • Re-validate quoted claims and numbers
  • Confirm field-specific terminology

Confidentiality Shield

Keep unpublished content out of public tools. Use protected environments when manuscript privacy is required.

Hard Rule

AI may support drafting and editing. AI may not be listed as author. Final scientific and ethical accountability remains human.
