04. Should I Use AI Here?

Back to Gallery

1. Define Task

Is AI assisting language, structure, coding, or visualization? Be explicit.

2. Check Policy

Read the target journal/publisher rules for this task.

3. Run Securely

Use tools that protect confidentiality and IP. Do not expose unpublished content.

4. Verify Output

Review for factual errors, bias, false citations, and plagiarism.

5. Declare Use

Add disclosure: tool, version, reason, and how the output was reviewed.

6. Final Human Review

GO: accurate, transparent, and compliant.

REWORK: weak citations or unclear disclosure.

STOP: policy or confidentiality breach.

Core principle: AI is assistive. Human authors remain responsible for the final manuscript.
